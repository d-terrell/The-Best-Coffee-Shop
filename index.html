Overall Drink Score

Methodology

The judging will be divided into three main categories and each rated on a 1 to 5 scale.

  • Drinks (1-5)
  • Menu (1-5)
  • Service(1-5)

The maxium overall drink score is 15 and the minimum possible score is 0.

1 - 5 Scale Meaning

  1. Nasty
  2. Meh
  3. Alright
  4. Pretty Good
  5. Delicious

Individual Drinks

In addition, there will be a drink specific subcategory in which I rate individual beverages on a 1 to 5 scale and directly compare same drinks across different coffee shops.

For each individual beverage, I will record the price, size in ounces, and then calculate the price per ounce.

Individually Scored Based On:

  • Balance (1-5)
  • Sweetness (1-5)
  • Flavor (1-5)
  • Originality (1-5)

The maxium individual drink score is 20 and the minimum possible score is 0.
